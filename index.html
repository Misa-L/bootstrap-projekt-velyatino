About our resort

Thermal Waters Velyatino is famous for its mineral-rich water with healing properties. The water temperature ranges from 36–38°C and helps relax muscles, improve circulation, and reduce stress. The complex features several thermal and freshwater pools, a salt room for respiratory health, and a sauna world including Finnish, Turkish, and eucalyptus options. Visitors can also enjoy hydromassage jets, professional massage treatments, and outdoor tubing.
